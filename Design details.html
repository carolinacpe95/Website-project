SuperChef

  • Description: after a few years at university, a group of friends and I decided to start our own business. The idea for Super Chef came about when we asked ourselves every time we went to a restaurant the same question, ‘Why don’t we know who the chef is?’ We realized how difficult it was for a chef to have the recognition that one deserves when operating behind a brand. Therefore, we created a virtual home delivery platform that instead of showing your customer restaurant brands, it showed them chef’s names who prepared their recipes from home and delivered them to their customers.
  • Project date: 2016-2017
